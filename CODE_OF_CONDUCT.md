# Code of Conduct

Jamarr should be a practical and respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be direct, constructive, and respectful.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when impact differs from intent.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the code, design, documentation, or operational tradeoff.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping issues and pull requests clear and scoped.
- Be patient with users and contributors who are learning the project.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Sexualised language or imagery where it is not relevant to the project.
- Publishing private information, credentials, logs, keys, or personal contact details without permission.
- Repeatedly pushing a topic after maintainers have made a moderation decision.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, restrict participation, or ban users when behaviour harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report conduct privately, contact the maintainer through the security contact in `SECURITY.md`. Include links, screenshots, or context where possible.

Reports will be handled with discretion. Retaliation against someone who reports a concern is not acceptable.
